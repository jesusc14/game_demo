Gold Team - Alex Hilwa, Andrew Howell, Jesus Celestino, and David Oganov

Pogo Frenzy


Description

Introducing our latest game in development: Pogo Frenzy! In this exciting game, you play as a pogo stick figure and must navigate through challenging levels by moving left to right across the screen. The levels are made up of a drawn-out platform, with various obstacles such as enemies trying to knock you off balance and end the game.

To defend yourself, you have a ground pound move that can eliminate enemies, or you can avoid them altogether by skillfully jumping over them. Your pogo stick figure is always bouncing and leaning towards the intended direction, making the game all the more thrilling. But beware, the enemies you encounter are not your ordinary foes. They include raindrops that can affect your balance, miniature erasers, and even a massive pencil that births more mini erasers and acts as the final boss of the game.

So, are you ready to embark on this pogo stick adventure and overcome all obstacles in your way? Stay tuned for updates and get ready to jump into action!
